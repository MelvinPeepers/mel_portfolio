I'm currently a support engineer at Callcentric, which is a BYOD (Bring Your Own Device) Voice Over Internet Protocol (VoIP) provider. I provide support for onboarding new customers, billing, product questions, debugging Session Initiation Protocol (SIP), helping individuals and companies around the world configure and manage their IP PBXes and hardware devices, troubleshooting issues with devices and networks. I also work with developers testing new user agents and SIP apps as well as creating guides for them.

Before Callcentric, I worked at Pace Analytical as a contract employee for 3M in Minnesota. I helped the 3M Environmental Lab create an archiving process for all their environmental lab reports. I also maintained and documented the archiving process

I enjoy working with customers. Listening to their problems and finding solutions to those problems. After learning software development I've discovered new ways I can help customers by improving existing products, adding new features, fixing bugs, and creating new products.
